# The Marked Edge Walk (MEW) for a Statistical Analysis of Minority Representation after Callais

This repository is the keepsake of Professor Daryl DeFord's Undergraduate Research Summer Institute group at Vassar College endeavors to analyze the future of Southern States post Louisiana v. Callais.

First of all, we would like to thank Atticus McWhorter for joining us in our summer research journey, who guided us alongside Professor DeFord on how to implement the Marked Edge Walk to run our analysis and teaching us how to program in Julia.

For a thorough walkthrough on the Mark Edge Walk, please read the following literature that Professor DeFord and Atticus contributed: https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.17714v2
